# What different terms mean

**Reach out**   
  
Reach out would be like if I send a message to a candidate on LI I’m interested in, or message to connect/reconnect with someone, or like responding to a candidates application

You might reachout a candidate two or three times before moving on

Anything after that initial touch point, isn’t a reachout

**“Sourcing Team Screen” Interview Type** 

We have a new interview type available called ‘**Sourcing Team Screen**’. This new enhancement will **not**impact any local team’s reports as your team’s will still be utilizing the ‘Recruiter Interview’ interview type. 

* **WHO**should use it?

This interview type was put into place to help our sourcing and pre\-interviewing recruiting teams better define and track their impact. The teams that will use this: Build’s Talent Engagement Team, Global Capabilities (Brittany Plumb’s team) and other country\-specific sourcing teams, ID\&E Program Managers and the teams who support their work. This is **NOT**intended to be used by teams outside of the ones listed. 

* **WHY**and **WHEN**will it be used?

Enables accurate measurement of our Slalom sourcing teams, success and ROI of events, and accurate conversion rates for our business. It also helps accurately measure event ROI (university, diversity, and any other recruiting events); how many candidates were met at the event and how many moved into recruiter screens for specific roles.  

**Sourcing Screen VS Recruiter Screen**

We will be using the **“Sourcing Screen”** for pipelining/networking purposes ONLY – any future talent pooling activities.   

Whilst a **“Recruiter Screen”** will also be used by the Talent Engagement team. The “Recruiter Screen” will be used for passive candidates sourced by the Talent Engagement team going through the end to end process with a Talent Acquisition Manager.

If you need further clarity or context, please feel free to reach out !  
  
**Evergreen Job Orders**

New Evergreen Networking Job Orders.

So the best practice for the TE team will be the following for accurate reporting:

**Sourcing Evaluation for Passive candidates:**

Candidates we are looking to **"Talent Pool"** or in our case **"Sourcing Screen Evaluation"** \- the process will be to add them to the right networking job order first and then add the evaluation. So **NOT** to add the evaluation first and then add them to networking job order **\<\- This will impact reporting \- \-**  if you do this we would need to email JobScience support and get them to change the sub\-status.   
  
It’s really important to apply to the JO first and then log your evaluation in the application for the JO  


 **Recruiter Evaluation for Direct candidates**

If we deem the candidate to be a direct introduction, in this case a **"Recruiter Evaluation"**\- we can then stay with the same process. So the TA Manager will apply the candidate to the right job order and there is no need to apply them to a Networking Job Order.
