# SOURCING - Engagement Screen - Process

 **Talent Sourcing Reach Out**

Prior to the phone call you would have messaged the candidate via LinkedIn Recruiter or another forum 

Some examples of the different styles of interaction: 

General candidate outreach 

Candidates impacted via change or layoff 

Candidates Actively looking for work 

 Warm conversation and have an ice breaker – Ask candidate if they’ve heard of Slalom before?  Find out what they are looking for career aspiration wise.

DIALOGUE below: 

*Hi XXX,*

*How has your day been? I saw on your linkedIn you recently went to a summit. How was it? Ohhh great … ← **Ice breaker***   
  
*So tell me what are you looking for next? What is your career aspiration?*   
  
Let the candidate speak and actively listen to what they have to say. 

Tie in with what the candidate is looking for at some stage in the introduction (for eg global company then emphasis of our growth).

Make sure you’re transparent with the process and let the candidate know this is more of an introduction to Slalom/Slalom Build.  
  
*Fantastic \- Have you heard of Slalom before?*   
  
If the candidate says “No” (majority of the time).Then say the following:   
  
*Great, let me set the scene and go through with you our process. It is very different to other organisations due to our growth. I would love in this conversation for you to understand a bit about Slalom and our growth. If we are aligned then I can take you through the formal recruitment process.* 

Give an introduction of Slalom and cover these areas.

 

**Overview of Slalom** – 

*Slalom is a modern consultancy focused on modern technology, strategy and business transformation. We are working with the latest technologies and partnered with companies such as AWS, Snowflake, etc. We are a gold partner with AWS, so a lot of our projects are AWS focussed currently here in Australia.* 

**Journey in Australia and Global growth** – 

 *From a global perspective we are a Global company \- Slalom’s HQ is in the US in Seattle \- we have 38\+ different markets across the US, UK and Canada. 12K\+ employees and doing some fantastic work with our clients.*   
  
*The leaders decided to bring the same concept over to Australia 2\.5 years ago and since we have gone on an exciting journey. Currently in Australia we are sitting at 300\+ people and working with Tier 1\-2 clients. From a global perspective there is also growth across other regions such as NZ and Germany, and we are planning on having more footprint across region.* 

**PAUSE** \- Ask candidate if they have any questions before you go through the Culture and Model.

**Culture** \-    
  
*We have won a lot of accolades such as Forbes 100, Fortune 100 etc*..*Our culture is heavily invested on people. We are privately listed and we have a bit of flex of not having shareholders or board members. A great example of this is that historically we’ve been able to withstand global changes and look after our people.*   
  
*The key here is creating a psychology safe environment for our people to come in, innovate and do what they love. If you had a chance to look at our glassdoor reviews, this gives you a great reflection of who we are. We really believe we can utilise technology to drive change and make a difference. We do heaps within the social impact space with a focus on various areas such as Diversity and Inclusion, and Climate change solution.*   
  
*Typically a learning culture/mindset opposed to just delivery. We have softer skills training rather than everything technically based. Eg. ERG mental health training, bias training, BuildU etc..*   
  
Give your own personal experience, how Slalom is an amazing place. Being authentic is the key! 

 

**Model** – not working with every client aligned to our values, differentiation and end to end of how the model works – PEM etc

*Our culture reflects our model to some degree. We don’t work with every client and selective on who with engage with (generally with clients with the same type of core values as us).*   
  
*Slalom is more focussed on the strategic side and Slalom Build is implementation focused.*   


*From your experience you would most likely fit in this area of the business XXXX which fits within Slalom Build.* 

  
*Slalom Build has 6 core capabilities \- SO, SE, PE, QE, DE and XD. Depending on what the client needs the capabilities will form a squad to build out the product for the client. We use a PEM methodology to make sure this is delivered. You are not working in silos and is a team based approach.*   
  
\- *We work in a partnership model with our clients, so once the project is completed we hand it over to the client and move on to the next project.*  
\- *Don’t work from the client site \- generally from home/Slalom office ( hybrid model and every team is different on when they come into the office to collaborate).*   
\- *We are focussed within our APAC market on projects and not an offshoring model* 

  
\- PAUSE and ASK if the candidate has any questions?   


**Recruitment process** **and next steps–** highlight it is different and the next steps (what the TA manager will go through and levelling with be determined in the interview process)    


*If you’re interested in what I’ve mentioned, I can take you through the rest of the recruitment process. So next steps I will send you an email and CC in the dedicated Talent Manager for the specific capability. We won’t be able to determine the levelling until the interview process stage. Next Step the dedicated Talent Manager will go through your background in a lot of depth and talk you through a bit more about the capability itself.*   
  
 *Appreciate your time and if you have any questions please let me know.*   
  
END
