# Interview Correspondence

The page contains the instructions for pre \& post interview emails along with samples of each. The initial screen and skills screen pages will provide guidance and links to this page.

**EMAIL 1**

* **Subject Line**: "Evaluation Packet with Resume"
* **Where it comes from?** [recruiting@slalom.com](mailto:recruiting@slalom.com)
* **When does it hit inbox?** Midnight the morning of the scheduled interview
* **Email Content:** First page is the resume, following pages include the feedback that is put into the system\& how we hire guidance (which can be ignored)
* **Disclaimer:** Once you reach the "How we Hire\- Guidance for Interviewers" \& pages below, please disregard that specific content. It pertains to our local recruiting framework.

**EMAIL 2**

* **Subject Line:** "Evaluation For (insert candidate name)"
* **Where does it come from?** [recruiting@slalom.com](mailto:recruiting@slalom.com)
* **When does it hit inbox?** The day of the scheduled interview
* **Email Content:** Includes link to submit written interview feedback
* **Disclaimer:**  There may be a delay on receiving this depending on when the TA representative enters this into the system.

 

**EMAIL 3**

* **Subject Line:** "Important! Evaluation for (insert candidate name) is Past Due"
* **Where does it comes from?** [recruiting@slalom.com](mailto:recruiting@slalom.com)
* **When does it hit inbox?** 24 hours after the interview takes place
* **Email Content:** Reminder to submit feedback and includes link to submit written feedback
* **Disclaimer:** This will reoccur each day until the evaluation has been completed. This is an excellent reminder to provide timely feedback to TA team, so we can maintain a great candidate experience with the candidate
