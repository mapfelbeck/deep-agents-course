# Sample Interviewing Questions  (Moved to Loop)

These questions are used in the Initial Interview and Technical Interview to get a sense of a software engineering candidate’s technical proficiency. Ultimately we are looking for a holistic view of the candidates proficiency \- these sample questions are organized in a way to represent the breadth of skills by each top\-level category in this page.

We’ve organized these sample questions to help align to those skill sets. Interviewers should understand the nuances of the role and that is being interviewed for. They can then draw from this question bank to ensure the appropriate aspects of the candidates experience are being evaluated.

An interviewer should seek evidence that the candidate can contribute to a team across all of the activities that we typically perform including analysis, design, development, testing, building \& deploying, and running high quality software.
