## **AI Tool Experience \& Strategy**

* "Walk me through how you'd approach a new codebase you've never seen before, using AI assistance"
* "Describe a time when AI gave you incorrect code. How did you identify and fix it?"
* "How would you explain to a client when AI assistance is worth the investment vs. traditional development?"

## **Technical Judgment**

* "What information do you typically include when asking an AI to help debug a performance issue?"
* "How do you decide what level of detail to include in comments when you know AI tools will be reading your code later?"

### Assessment

Each assessment should include a summary of the candidate’s AI assisted coding skills and their overall strength/suitability for a AI\-Enable Team Leadership role, e.g.:

\[Candidate] is a \[novice/intermediate/strong/expert] in \[AI\-enabled software development skill, e.g. “context engineering”].
