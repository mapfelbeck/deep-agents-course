# Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team

**Charter and Mission**:

A pillar of Slalom Build’s growth strategy is dependent on its interviewing process. As we continue to scale our interviewing process across the network, the Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team is established with a core set of principles and responsibilities to ensure quality, consistency, and maintenance of our software engineering ***Initial Interviews and Technical Interviews***. This group only focuses on the engineering interview process, and is not focused on behavioral interviews, closes, or the overall recruiting process. The Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team exists to keep our engineering interview culture laser\-focused on identifying and recommending to hire top talent to make us a better, more diverse, and stronger organization. The Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team will be made up of a varied team spanning Build Centers, Build Edge Locations, educational backgrounds, and professional experience.

Note: at this time, the Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team is focused on North America Build location. 

**Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team Groups Responsibilities**

Listed below are the responsibility of the Software Engineering Interviewing Team with aim and intent to ensure consistency, quality and maintenance of our Initial Interviews and Technical Interviews across our Build Locations.

Specific responsibilities include:

* Ensure our initial interviewers and technical interviewers:


	+ Are consistent across Build in their training, interview execution and documentation
	+ Can successfully identify talent
	+ Support a diverse set of Engineering talent and background
	+ Provide a positive experience for candidates
* Periodic reviews (and/or updates) of


	+ technical and behavioral interview write\-ups for quality, consistency and fairness
	+ training materials for conducting initial interviews and technical interviews
	+ training conducted to interviewers at each Build Locations
	+ data for trends, insights and patterns
* Partner with Talent Acquisition team to drive changes and improvements across the initial interview and technical interview process
* Participate and identify Build leaders and Location Based Champions to ensure initial interview summaries are reviewed for quality, consistency and fairness

**Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team Make up**

Listed below is the criteria of the team make up. The intent is to have a tenured and diverse team.

Who can be in the Software Engineering Interviewing Champion Team

* Software Engineering: Sr. Director or tenured Director
* Talent Acquisition: Director or tenured Sr. Manager

Software Engineering Interviewing Champion team group considerations

* Time zone representation \- team should represent each of our Build Location timezone
* Representation of each type of build location (Center, Edge, other)
* Membership diversity (gender, race, professional experience, … )
* Group size five to seven to mirror a scrum team

 

Tour of duty

* Recommend minimum one\-year tour of duty
* No more than 1 – 2 members can change per quarter after year commitment is achieved
* Membership is voluntary and can be self\-initiated
* Members may also recommend others to join
