# Technical Interview / Feedback Template [ARCHIVE]

* The purpose of this template is to create a more streamlined process, drive consistency, and prepare us with more detailed feedback to reference if we re\-interview candidates in the future
* For each category, there is a drop\-down box to choose a rating on how the candidate scored in that category

Please be sure to utilize our updated rating scale:



| **Rating** | **Meaning** |
| --- | --- |
| Not Covered | You didn't talk about the subject, or the subject was not applicable. (If giving this rating, please include a brief phrase why, i.e. "Not covered (ran out of time)" |
| Poor | Consistently and/or significantly missed expectations for the role for which they are interviewing. This rating is also applicable if the candidate has no knowledge in an area. |
| Fair | Sometimes met and sometimes missed expectations for the role for which they are interviewing |
| Good | Consistently met and sometimes exceeded expectations for the role for which they are interviewing |
| Excellent | Consistently and/or significantly exceeded expectations for the role for which they are interviewing |



---

Once your evaluation is complete, and you have your feedback summary write\-up ready, copy and paste the completed write\-up into the evaluation form in Jobscience and fill out the couple of additional fields, before submitting:

* **INTERVIEW SUMMARY section**


	+ **Interview Details**
	
	
		- This is the text field where you can paste the entire text of your feedback summary write\-up
	+ **Overall Recommendation**
	
	
		- There are 4 options in this dropdown (Hire, Leaning Hire, Leaning No Hire, No Hire)
		- However, in Build, we ask that everyone only use the **HIRE** or **NO\-HIRE** options
		
		
			* HIRE: Proceed with the interview process
			* NO\-HIRE: Do Not Proceed with the interview process
	+ **Overall Recommendation comments**
	
	
		- Just put the text "**N/A**" here
		
		
			* This is a required field in Jobscience, but we do not use it as part of the Build interview process
* **CBI RELATED QUESTIONS**


	+ You can IGNORE this entire section, we DO NOT fill out or use this section as part of the BUILD interview process

Reminder to not include any candidate PII (Personally Identifiable Information \- that could be used to identify, contact or locate an individual) in your feedback. [Link to PII training](https://rise.articulate.com/share/ilwziedjqh98CncqQydOlfTqiaQHt63w#/)

* Candidate name​
* Telephone numbers​
* Home address​
* Education history​
* Date and place of birth​
* Social security or national insurance number​
* Nationality \& ethnicity​
* Photograph​
* Mother’s maiden name​
* Past, current, or compensation expectations​
* Driver’s license or government issues id number​
* Gender
