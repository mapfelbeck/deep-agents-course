# (SE) Software Engineering Interviewing  (Moved to Loop)

When interviewing Software Engineering roles, we are interested in identifying folks who are a fit with our vision and direction as a discipline.

# Why we exist

As a discipline, we accelerate the application of software engineering expertise at any scale to build better tomorrows for all…

# How we behave

We are seeking team members who exhibit these behaviors, and the interviewing process is our first step toward identifying the right folks and giving them the opportunity to bring their talents to our team. We don’t expect folks to be perfect in each of these areas, but we do seek these behaviors to ensure the best alignment to what we do and how we deliver excellence for our clients. 

## Technical Proficiency

We are skilled at developing high\-quality software using efficient design, development and test techniques

## Consulting Presence

We are supportive and solution\-focused as we thoughtfully challenge the status quo with our clients and partners.

## Persistence and Curiosity

We combine relentless problem\-solving with an insatiable curiosity to improve and innovate in service of building better solutions faster.

## Team Collaboration

We are the backbone of collaborative and inclusive delivery teams; we invest in ensuring each others' well\-being.

## Continuous Growth

We proactively invest in growing ourselves, our capability and the organization.

## Broad Awareness

We actively engage and radiate what is happening across the capability, build locations, slalom, the industry and our clients.

# What We Do

We build together to deliver business value.

We partner with clients, markets and across capabilities to **efficiently advise, analyze, design, develop, test, deploy, and run high\-quality software** applications, systems and components
