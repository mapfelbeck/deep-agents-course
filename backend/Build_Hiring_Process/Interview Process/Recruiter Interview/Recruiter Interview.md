# Recruiter Interview

# Overview

When there is a hiring need, Recruiters and discipline Leaders connect to understand the role(s) we are looking to hire. They cover off on level, location, specific skillsets needed and timeline of hire.

The recruiter then starts to fill the candidate pipeline with qualified individuals by doing extensive research in the market, asking for and following up on referrals, posting jobs, checking the incoming applicants and doing external sourcing by reaching out on LinkedIn or other sourcing technical tools, hosting and or going to networking events, and connecting with our established partnerships and community groups. 

The recruiter then will qualify the candidate further via a face to face, phone or virtual meeting known as the ‘Recruiter Interview’

# Coordination

Once a candidate responds with interest, the recruiter will set up at 30 min \- 1 hour long conversation to connect directly with the candidate. This is done via in\-person, zoom/MSFT Teams video call or phone call.

# Conversation

The goal of this first conversation is to qualify candidates, introduce and increase candidate interest in Slalom Build, answer initial questions and determine and set clear next steps. Recruiters can find specific questions and prompts [on our MSFT teams site here](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/file/BBC9B500-D0DC-4543-8BAE-40E551F720F1?tenantId=9ca75128-a244-4596-877b-f24828e476e2&fileType=pptx&objectUrl=https%3A%2F%2Ftwodegrees1.sharepoint.com%2Fsites%2FXMDRecruiting%2FShared%20Documents%2FGeneral%2FTraining%2FRecruiter%20-%20New%20Hire%20Training%20Material%2FOnboarding%2FRecruiter%20Interview%20Structure%20-%20Training.pptx&baseUrl=https%3A%2F%2Ftwodegrees1.sharepoint.com%2Fsites%2FXMDRecruiting&serviceName=teams&threadId=19:a680b01811304cba9695db750ac2ca05@thread.skype&groupId=f1738458-0745-46d0-8777-36631136aeff).

* Introduction


	+ Build rapport, set agenda and expectations of call
* Validate how familiar they are with Slalom Build


	+ Were they referred? Did they apply? Did they do research
* Dig into background of candidate


	+ Understand the candidates work history
	+ Have they worked on a team or in an individual contributor role (what size teams, companies, etc.)
	+ Roles and responsibilities they personally have had on projects
	+ Understand what specific skills or technologies they know
	+ What methodologies they are familiar with
* Dig into Slalom Core Values Match:


	+ Are they collaborative, learners vs. knowers, curious, humble, passionate about tech, etc.?
	+ Would this person be independent enough to operate with minimal supervision in a remote environment?
* Ask any additional qualifying questions


	+ Identify pain points of current situation
	+ Identify areas of interest/passion/what they are looking for
	+ Timeline \- are they passive or actively interviewing
	+ Authorization to work within that country
	+ Travel
	+ Compensation
* Give Slalom Build Pitch


	+ Our history, model, growth, career framework, clients we work with, project based work examples, collaborative culture, work life balance, IDE strategy and focus, fun environment!
* Set expectations for next steps


	+ Proceed to next step (explain our initial skills screen, get availability)
	+ or “Break up”
