# 1. Designer • Evaluation Criteria

### **STEP THREE:** Identify where you want to dig deeper in the next steps. Take note that it is NOT expected that someone will check all boxes. If they are exceeding many of the expectations provided here, consider evaluating them as a **Senior Designer**



| **Designer Summary** | **Outward Facing Job Title:** “Product Designer” |
| --- | --- |
| * ***Must*** **have some professional design experience** (whether in a production setting, freelance, or adjacent design stack). * Could be an early design career pivot (\< 2\-3 years) / bootcamp graduate. * Demonstrates an understanding of design fundamentals and solid UI/visual skills * Understands digital product design and how to work with tooling.  **Please Note:** Designer candidates should mostly come from our internship program, with some exceptions. Considerations include but are not limited to team size \& support in the market. | |

:frame\_photo:1f5bc🖼\#F4F5F7**Reminder about portfolio \& skills screens:** 

* Transferable skills from marketing, environmental, architectural, industrial, or other related fields of design and engineering experience should be considered. Look for potential in their ability to solve problems through design over proof of specific deliverables.
* Portfolio materials can be in many forms including but not limited to website, pdf, presentation, dribble, behance, social platform feed, etc. Consider whether they have used good judgement, attention to detail, and curation in the demonstration of work.
