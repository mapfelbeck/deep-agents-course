# Experience Design Candidate Evaluations (Moved to SP ✅)

These are our guidelines for how to begin an evaluation for all candidates from levels 1\-4\. In the nested page tree you will find overall XD Evaluation Criteria for reviews and screenings per each level.



---

### **STEP ONE:**

Please review the guidelines below while reviewing **all** of the provided materials which may include a Resume and/or LinkedIn page, Portfolio Website or Document.



| **Overall Evaluation Guidance** | | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *Look for **Growth mindset:*** Do they show interest in learning, taking on new responsibilities or expanding their skills over time? | *Look for **Positive impact*****:** Will they add to or bring a new dimension of experience and perspective to the organization? Do they embody Slalom values / show alignment with Build’s mission? | *Look for **Potential over pedigree:*** Place value on what they *can* do, not just what they have done. *For example:* Signs of advanced abilities in self\-management, problem\-solving, and teamwork may indicate that an individual can grow or fill gaps quickly. |
| *over* **A specific degree or** **educational background**, and avoid critiquing someone based on **longer employment history** or **gaps in their resume** | *over* **“Culture Fit”**, vaguely. It’s easy to fall into assuming someone is qualified because they remind us of ourselves. Instead, consider what you foresee them bringing to the community and why. | *over* **Proof someone has an 100% match for skills and past experience**, and avoid assuming having certain academic qualifications or working at a specific employer is qualification enough. |

### **STEP TWO:**

Look for these high\-level indicators to confirm you are evaluating at the correct level:



| **Design Intern** | **Designer** | **Senior Designer** | **Architect** | **Senior Architect** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Design Fundamentals    Product Delivery Skills     Discovery Skills    Self Mgmt / Autonomy    Client Mgmt    Leadership | Design Fundamentals    Product Delivery Skills     Discovery Skills    Self Mgmt / Autonomy    Client Mgmt    Leadership | Design Fundamentals    Product Delivery Skills     Discovery Skills    Self Mgmt / Autonomy    Client Mgmt     Leadership | Design Fundamentals    Product Delivery Skills     Discovery Skills    Self Mgmt / Autonomy    Client Mgmt     Leadership | Design Fundamentals    Product Delivery Skills     Discovery Skills    Self Mgmt / Autonomy    Client Mgmt     Leadership |
| Expected to bring strong design and conceptual skills that will allow them to learn how to support a delivery with oversight.***\*\*NOTE:** Intern evaluation criteria is not part of this material, this is for leveling clarification only.* | Expected to contribute to design activities within a discovery / delivery with oversight. | Expected to support the XD responsibilities of product delivery pod autonomously and be contribute to discovery activities with some oversight. | Expected to drive XD for product discovery and delivery activities autonomously. (Not expected to lead multiple workstreams or large programs, a heavy client\-management / complex discovery). | Expected to drive XD in discoveries and multi\-pod deliveries, setting direction for and overseeing other designers. |

 ***Important Note:** Transferable skills from marketing, environmental, architectural, industrial or other fields of design \& design\-adjacent experience should be considered through the Architect level.*
