# 4. Senior Architect, XD • Evaluation Criteria

### **STEP THREE:**Identify where you want to dig deeper in the next steps. Take note that it is NOT expected that someone will check all boxes. If they are exceeding many of the expectations provided here, flag to the recruiter that they should be evaluated as **Senior Principal**.



| **Senior Architect Summary:** | **Outward\-facing Job Title:** “Senior Product Design Lead” |
| --- | --- |
