# Candidate experience variances

We strive to maintain a consistent process for our hiring candidates. The overarching experience is consistent regardless of what capability the candidate is considering. As candidates are pursued for architect or leader roles, there are additional steps that are added to the overall process in order to ensure that the candidates for these advanced roles are a good technical and behavioral fit for slalom.
