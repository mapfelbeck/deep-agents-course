# Interview Process

The hiring process is as much about creating a good candidate experience as it is about finding candidates who share Slalom’s core values.

The build hiring process is encapsulated in the following candidate experience which is used to quickly assess and land candidates who are excited to join Slalom Build.

The candidate experience will have nuance based on capability that they are interviewing for, but the experience should carry some consistency to the following stages throughout. In each step along the way, it is the recruiter and interviewers shared responsibility to make the candidate feel comfortable and welcome while exploring their expertise and determining whether they share our values!
