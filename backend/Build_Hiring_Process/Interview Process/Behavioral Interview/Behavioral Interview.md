# Overview

The Behavioral Interview takes the candidate into a conversation in order to determine if the candidate would fit the characteristics that we look for as ‘ideal team players’. While the Initial Interview and the Technical Interview are focused primarily on Technical Proficiency, the Behavioral Screen looks to assess a candidates' non\-technical ability. 

From a CDF perspective, much of the Behavioral conversation is centered around the Deliver Exceptionally, Lead, and Grow Slalom competencies, whereas the Initial and Technical Interviews are used to assess a candidates ability in the Grow Expertise and Deliver Exceptionally competencies.
