# Personas

These definitions provide guidance for expectations that each individual will likely have as they participate in the hiring process.



| **Persona** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| Engineer Candidate | * Approximately 0\-7 years of experience * Individual contributor role * Engineer or Senior Engineer |
| SA/Leader Candidate | * Approximately 7\+ years of experience * Individual contributor with deep expertise or leader role * Architect, Sr. Architect, Sr. Principal or above |
| Recruiter | * Owns and manages projects for sourcing and guiding candidates through this process |
| Interviewer | * Assesses fit with role based on either Technical or Behavioral characteristics |
| Technical Interviewer | * Assesses technical fit * Assesses professional and communication skills * A Senior Engineer, Architect, or Senior Architect for initial screens * A Sr. Principal, Director or Senior Director for in\-depth screens (skills screen or behavioral) * It is preferred the the technical interviewer is the slated People Manager |
| Behavioral Interviewer | * Assesses team fit * Assesses collaboration, drive and ability to execute in delivery * A Sr. Principal, Director, or Senior Director |
| People Manager | * Resource to whom the Candidate will report * Capable of measuring professional and communication skills * A Sr. Principal, Director, or Senior Director |
| Closer | * Resource who extends offers to candidates * Director or above for Engineer/Sr. Engineer Candidates * Senior Director or above for senior\-level (Architect\+) candidates |
