# Roles and Responsibilities

  




   | Responsibility | Role: Hiring Managers | Role: Recruiters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Candidate Sourcing | * Provide Role Description * Refine with Recruiter feedback * Provide Hiring Plan | * Create sourcing strategy based on hiring manager input * Facilitate weekly candidate pipeline review * Attend weekly staffing to understand demand * Provide feedback on what is and is not working / available |
| Interview Scheduling | * Provide recruiters calendar access including travel details * Provide consistent availability * Provide backfill if unavailable | * Calendar interviews quickly * Provide all participants visibility into all scheduled interviews * Provide resumes, role, economics, reports\-to, and closing in each meeting invite * Detail which questions interviewer is answering |
| Interviewing | * Understand resumes, role, economics, reports\-to, and closing * Understand next steps and questions to be answered * Document a move forward decision | * Coordinate physical logistics of in person or virtual meetings * Double check that Interviewer has necessary information * Make sure that outcome is documented and candidate moves forward or out |
| Closing | * Only start this conversation if we are going to extend an offer * Understand resumes, role, economics, reports\-to, and closing * Give Verbal offer and Selling Messages | * Ensure closing interviews happen as quickly and frequently as possible * Ensure that Closer is at the right level * Ensure that the Closer has the necessary background, economic, and interview feedback information to get the candidate to say “Yes I will join Slalom.” |
| Extending Offers | * Communicate Offer to Recruiter * Be available to quickly answer follow on questions or negotiation points | * Document the offer that the Hiring Manager extended as quickly as possible * Get Hiring Manager’s approval of offer * Extend offer and facilitate / guide communication and negotiation as needed |
