If your capability manages a bank of questions in a capability\-specific space, you should be able to have those questions show up in the above sections by adding the appropriate labels to the page(s). Following are the labels to use:

### Interview\-type Labels

* `initial-interview-questions`
* `skills-interview-questions`
* `behavioral-interview-questions`

### Capability Labels

* `capability-cds`: Cloud, DevOps, Security
* `capability-de`: Data Engineering
* `capability-qe`: Quality Engineering
* `capability-se`: Software Engineering
* `capability-so`: Solution Ownership
* `capability-xd`: Experience Design
