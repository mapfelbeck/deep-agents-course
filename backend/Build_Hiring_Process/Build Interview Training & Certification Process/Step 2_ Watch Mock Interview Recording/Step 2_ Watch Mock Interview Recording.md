# Step 2: Watch Mock Interview Recording

Build TA has created a library of discipline\-specific mock interview videos from our very own experienced, Build interviewers! The purpose of these videos is to get introduced to the typical format for each level and interview stage. Also, anyone can access the video library at any time if you ever need a refresher.

* You are only required to watch one video per interview stage for the discipline you are aligned to.
* If you choose, you may watch all videos for your discipline. However, please note you will only be interviewing for roles below your current level.
