# Build Interview Training & Certification Process

Welcome Build Leaders! Whether you are a new hire, a new promote, or a leader that has been here awhile and hasn’t yet been trained on our Build Interviewing Process, this page is for you.

Please see below for the steps to take in order to become a Build Certified Interviewer. If you have any specific questions, please reach out to **slalombuildinterviewertraining@slalom.com**. 

## **Build Interviewer Training \& Certification Process**

### **Eligibility:**

* Must be a **Sr. Principal\+** to become a certified interviewer
* Must be an employee for **a minimum of 3 months** prior to beginning interview training
* Must be an employee for **a minimum of 6 months** prior to interviewing candidates on your own

### **Certification Process Overview:**

 Follow this simple certification process to help create the best candidate experience and be a partner to TA!  

 *Steps 1\-4 must be completed within a 6\-month period.*  

#### <https://slalom.atlassian.net/l/cp/uF3NkwAT>

* This one hour training will provide a general introduction and high\-level overview of our interview process for all capabilities and include a Q\&A sector.
* Held quarterly and led by Build TA team member

#### <https://slalom.atlassian.net/l/cp/MnqERXMw>

* This step is to be completed for **EACH**interview stage (Initial Interview, Technical Interview, and Behavioral Interview)
* You as the interviewer may watch all video recordings if you would like, however, it is not required
* You have the ability to revisit these recordings if you ever need a refresher

* This step is to be completed for **EACH**interview stage (Initial Interview, Technical Interview, and Behavioral interview)
* TA team will be tracking where you are in the process and what needs to be completed and proactively tag you to interviews.

#### <https://slalom.atlassian.net/l/cp/NL8PrkuM>

* This step is to be completed for **EACH**interview stage (Initial Interview, Technical Interview, and Behavioral Interview)
* The TA team will work with schedules to ensure you have a certified interviewer shadowing you

### **Things to Note:**

* TA recommends that while completing Steps 2\-4 outlined above, you get exposure across interviewing different levels/personas.

*Example:*  A Principal interviewer watches one mock interview recording for a Senior Engineer, but shadows 1 live interview \+ conducts 1 live interview for an Architect or Senior Architect.   

* Peer to peer interviews are not conducted within our Build Interview Hiring Process, so it is required that YOU get trained to interview for roles below your current level.

### **Once Steps 1\-4 are complete, you are officially a Certified Build Interviewer!  🎉**

* You will be added to the Build Interviewer Matrix for the interview stage(s) you became certified for and can start conducting those interviews on your own!
* TA tracks how many interviews you conduct on a 2 week basis and works hard to not overload you with more than 2 in that time frame.
* To keep an eye on your certification progress, check out the [Uncertified Principal\+](https://app.powerbi.com/groups/me/apps/37d54f4a-5609-4d0b-b1ef-3ebcfb5c37c1/reports/2f3ab004-f175-439a-b5b3-a215b8c81628/ReportSectioncf70ec200d592942060d?experience=power-bi) page on our Build Interviewer Matrix.

If you would like to note who is a certified interviewer for your discipline, check out the [Interviewer Search](https://app.powerbi.com/groups/me/apps/37d54f4a-5609-4d0b-b1ef-3ebcfb5c37c1/reports/2f3ab004-f175-439a-b5b3-a215b8c81628/ReportSection9dc7a314c14e1bb5a061?experience=power-bi) page on our Build Interviewer Matrix.
