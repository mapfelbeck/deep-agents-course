# Networking Interview Guide

Hi Build Leaders/Interviewers! Due to our current hiring posture, we are conducting a lot of networking calls as a way to continue to meet and engage with candidates and tell our Slalom Build story. Build TA has created a networking guide for you all to use as a reference point if you are scheduled to take these networking calls: 

### **What is a Leader Networking Interview?**

* Conversation specific to connecting with the candidate on a capability
* Instead of interviewing the candidate for skills, we’re listening to what their passionate about and the work they want to do
* Sharing back information about the discipline, how it operates, how projects are staffed, and the Slalom Build experience

### **Leader Networking Interview Breakdown:**

* Introductions **(5 min)**
* Candidate Focused: Motivators, Skills, Career Goals, Application Status, Event Interest **(10 min)**
* Discipline Pitch \& Storytelling **(5 min)**
* Candidate Questions **(5 min)**
* Wrap Up / Set Expectations **(5 min)**

### **Evaluation Feedback Template:**

Follow the feedback template below to help us drive consistency \& capture the right information about a candidate in the Jobscience evaluation.

**Introductions:**

* Did the candidate ask any notable questions?
* Were you \& the candidate aligned on purpose?

**Candidate Focus:**

* Notable details about their skillset/career experience *Ex. People management or architecture skills*
* Do you think they fit the "Builder" profile \& your discipline?
* Did you identify any specific motivators or career goals?
* What's your estimation on their leveling?

**Discipline Focus/Build Pitch:**

* What resonated with them the most? *Ex. Project stories, development budget, ERGs*

**Wrap Up:**

* Is there anything for the recruiter to follow up on? *Ex. Candidate mentioned that they're actively looking*
* Potential gaps? *Ex. More of a Local than Build fit? Does not align to discipline skillset?*
* Anything to know when we re\-engage? *Ex. Skills you recommended they grow in*
