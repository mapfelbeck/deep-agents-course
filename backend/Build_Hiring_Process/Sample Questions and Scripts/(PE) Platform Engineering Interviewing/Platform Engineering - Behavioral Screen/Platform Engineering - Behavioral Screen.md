# Platform Engineering - Behavioral Screen

The behavioral screen presents a unique opportunity within our Platform Engineering evaluation process to delve into a candidate's responses to diverse situations, allowing us to assess their approach to the types of work and environments inherent in our field.

Contained within this set are sample behavioral questions aligned with the competencies and skills outlined in our Career Development Framework. It is crucial to recognize that not all questions need to be used in an interview; rather, the goal is to select a tailored subset that corresponds to the specific role and level under consideration.

Our existing process integrates two behavioral interviews, and it is imperative that the interviewers collaborate in advance to curate a set of sample questions that cover a spectrum of competencies and skills. This collaborative effort ensures a comprehensive evaluation of the candidate. It is essential to acknowledge that the perspective and experiences of an early career individual contributor differ from those of a seasoned leader.

To optimize outcomes, revisit this set of sample questions regularly to familiarize yourself with the behavioral qualities we seek. This familiarity aids in determining whether a candidate is well\-suited for the role they are interviewing for. During the interview, invest time in engaging with the candidate, bearing in mind that the primary goal is to leave with a robust assessment of their potential success at Slalom in the role in question. Evaluators must gauge the candidate's ability to contribute effectively within a dynamic team environment characterized by diverse viewpoints and competing priorities.

## Sample Behavior Questions \- Level: Leader



|  | **Category** | **Question** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1\. | **Conflict Management** | Narrate a situation where conflicts arose within your team. Share the steps you took to resolve the conflict, highlighting your approach to fostering a positive resolution and maintaining team cohesion. |
| 2\. | **Daily Operations** | Provide a detailed account of your typical day\-to\-day responsibilities and activities. How do you organize and prioritize your tasks to ensure efficiency in daily operations? |
| 3\. | **Learning from Setbacks** | Reflect on a project that encountered significant challenges. Share the specific setbacks, your role in addressing them, and the key lessons you derived from the experience. |
| 4\. | **Task Breakdown** | Discuss your methodology for breaking down tasks and assigning them to team members. Provide an example of a situation where effective task breakdown contributed to successful project completion. |
| 5\. | **Empowering Team Members** | Illustrate how you empower and delegate responsibilities to your team members. Share a specific example where your delegation approach resulted in team members' growth and increased efficiency. |
| 6\. | **Team Challenge Strategies** | Detail specific strategies you employ to challenge and motivate your team. Share an instance where your motivational tactics positively impacted team performance. |
| 7\. | **Conflict Resolution** | Explain a situation where you had to handle conflicts, both internal and external to your team. Discuss the steps you took to resolve the conflicts, emphasizing the impact of your approach on team dynamics. |
| 8\. | **Client Resolution** | Share a scenario where you had to handle conflicts with clients. Explain your approach to resolving client conflicts and how you ensured a positive client relationship. |
| 9\. | **Building Builders** | Elaborate on your approach to empowering others to grow. Share a specific example where you played a role in fostering the professional development of team members. |
| 10\. | **Building the Craft** | Explain your approach to learning new technologies. Provide an example of a time when you successfully adopted and integrated a new technology into your skill set, highlighting the steps you took in the learning process. |

## Sample Behavior Questions \- Level: IC



|  | **Category** | **Question** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1\. | **Building the Craft** | Explain your approach to learning new technologies. Provide an example of a time when you successfully adopted and integrated a new technology into your skill set, highlighting the steps you took in the learning process. |
| 2\. | **Autonomy and Accountability** | Describe a project where you had a high level of autonomy. How did you manage your responsibilities, and what steps did you take to ensure accountability for the outcomes? |
| 3\. | **Collaboration and Knowledge Sharing** | Share a situation where collaboration and knowledge sharing were crucial to project success. How did you contribute to the collaborative process, and what steps did you take to share knowledge within your team? |
| 4\. | **Problem\-Solving Approach** | Discuss a specific challenge you encountered in your role. How did you approach problem\-solving, and what steps did you take to overcome the obstacle? |
| 5\. | **Initiative and Innovation** | Provide an example of a situation where you took initiative or introduced an innovative idea that positively impacted a project or task. How did your initiative contribute to the overall success? |
| 6\. | **Feedback and Adaptability** | Describe a scenario where you received constructive feedback on your work. How did you incorporate that feedback, and how adaptable were you in making necessary adjustments? |
| 7\. | **Project Efficiency** | Share an experience where you enhanced the efficiency of a project or process. What specific actions did you take to streamline operations and contribute to the project's success? |
| 8\. | **Mentoring or Peer Support** | Discuss a time when you provided mentorship or support to a colleague or peer. How did your guidance contribute to their development or the success of a project? |
| 9\. | **Work Ethic and Time Management** | Illustrate a situation where you demonstrated a strong work ethic and effective time management. How did you ensure the timely completion of tasks and maintain a high standard of work? |
| 10\. | **Quality Improvement** | Share an example of how you contributed to improving the quality of deliverables or processes in your role. What specific measures did you take to ensure a higher standard of output? |
