# PE Technical Leveling

## Leveling guidance

We use the same leveling system provided by the [Career Development Framework (CDF)](https://twodegrees1.sharepoint.com/teams/Build/SitePages/Career-Framework.aspx) for interview candidates as we use for our employees. The technical level mapping to CDF can be found here.

We recognize that candidates come from a wide variety of backgrounds regarding education, development experience, professional experience, opportunity, and others. This variety means that each candidate will present differently on our evaluation scale. It is therefore important to evaluate a candidates technical background holistically.

The following guidance is provided to evaluate at what level a candidate would fit on our scale regardless of background. The rating scale used in this chart are documented here.

\*Note that candidates are individual and are always treated on a case by case basis. The goal here is to provide a consistent framework as a guide.



| **Level** | **Technical Evaluation Categories** | **Criteria** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Engineer | Fundamentals, Cloud, DevOps, IaC | Score a Good or better in 2 categories |
| Senior Engineer | Fundamentals, Cloud, DevOps, IaC, Troubleshooting | Score a Good or better in 3 categories |
| Architect | Fundamentals, Cloud, DevOps, IaC, Security, Troubleshooting | Score a Good or better in 4 categories. |
| Sr. Architect | Fundamentals, Cloud, DevOps, IaC, Security, Troubleshooting | Score a Good or better in 5 categories. |
| Sr. Principal | Fundamentals, Cloud, DevOps, IaC, Security, Troubleshooting | Score a Good or better in 5 categories. |
