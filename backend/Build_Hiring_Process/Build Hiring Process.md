# Build Hiring Process

# Hi!

Chances are you want to prepare for doing a recruiting interview. Great! We have some excellent resources here that will help you along your way:

* The Interview Process will give you a complete, holistic picture of the candidate experience \- regardless of capability. You can go here for information on how interviews of different types are set up and coordinated as well as for guidelines for how to run a particular type of interview
* Interviewing Tools is a great place to go get guidance for how to use the tools necessary for running an interview \- go here for information on how to use our Whiteboarding tools and our candidate tracking tools.
* Sample Questions is the meat and potatoes for a library of questions that each capability regularly curates in order to represent the qualities that we are looking for in a candidate

There are other resources here that will help you gain a deeper understanding for our philosophy behind recruiting and hiring. Our ability to deliver great solutions for our clients starts with great people. We have worked across capabilities to develop an approach that helps us identify people who are passionate about the work they do while being competitive in a continuously evolving market.
